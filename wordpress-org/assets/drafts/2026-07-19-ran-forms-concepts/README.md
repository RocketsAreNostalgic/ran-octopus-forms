# RAN Forms artwork studies — 19 July 2026

These are original RocketAreNostalgic visual studies for **RAN EmailOctopus for
Jetpack Forms**. They are drafts only: do not upload them to WordPress.org or
replace the approved assets in `../../` until a direction has been selected and
reviewed.

Each direction has the standard WordPress.org directory raster sizes:

- `banner-1544x500.png`
- `banner-772x250.png`
- `icon-256x256.png`
- `icon-128x128.png`

`concept-banner.png` and `concept-icon.png` are the higher-resolution source
studies retained for later crops or refinement.

## Directions

- `signal-bureau/` — Octopus signal operator routing forms to a destination
  station. This was the user's preferred wide concept.
- `form-mapper/` — Brass mapping console with multiple routes and destinations.
- `celestial-router/` — Octopus rocket observatory following form constellations
  to a distant beacon.
